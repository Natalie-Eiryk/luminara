A red fox resting in dappled sunlight beneath flowering trees, impressionist oil painting style.
Visible, energetic brushstrokes creating texture and movement across the canvas. Fox rendered
with loose, suggestive strokes rather than precise detail - burnt orange and vermillion dabs
suggest fur, quick white strokes capture light catching the tail. Broken color technique with
pure pigments placed side-by-side: violet shadows mixing optically with orange sunlight,
yellow-green grass blades as individual dashed strokes. Soft atmospheric perspective with
background trees dissolving into pale blue and lavender haze. Dappled light pattern created
through alternating warm (cadmium yellow) and cool (cobalt blue) strokes. Pastel spring color
palette: soft pinks, pale greens, warm ochres, sky blues. Joyful, ephemeral atmosphere capturing
a fleeting moment. Canvas texture visible, impasto highlights, en plein air spontaneous quality,
signed with loose calligraphy in corner.
